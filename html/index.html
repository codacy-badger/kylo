Welcome!

Welcome to Kylo, probably the most unconstrained pure survival server. Here, your goal is to live. The absence of any protective plug-ins means that you must ensure your own safety and get out of the spawnpoint. Then, everything is handed over to you, do whatever you want!

Kylo Server Config

Updated on 2/29/2020

Kylo's main server consists of a dual E5 processor, so each player can build large redstone machinery without worrying about server lag. The Kylo maintenance team has also deployed dedicated line acceleration nodes on all continents around the world, and Kylo's intelligent analysis system ensures that you can play stably and smoothly in any corner of the world.
